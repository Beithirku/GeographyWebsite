Japan

Japan is a beautiful country home to some amazing scenery and culture. The capital of Japan is Tokyo (東京都), Tokyo is located in the Kanto region of Japan it has 23 special wards, 26 cities, 1 district, and 4 subprefectures the city is known for it's shopping districts, for example Shibuya which is also home to the famous Shibuya crossing where thousands of people cross to go to work on a daily basis this crossing was also seen in the movie Tokyo Drift from the Fast and the Furious series.

Japan is also home to Mount Fuji in the Chubu region, which is around a two hour car ride from Tokyo. Mount Fuji is surrounding by beautiful forests one of which being the Aokigahara (青木ヶ原) otherwise known as the Sea of Trees (樹海 Jukai) or the Suicide Forest the forest is located at the Northwest flank of Mount Fuji.

Japan has some of the highest snowfall in the world it’s mainly northern Japan that gets hit by heavy snow though unlike the United Kingdom this doesn’t affect daily life, it’s dealt with almost immediately, creating walls of snow by the road. four of Japans cities are also listed in top ten’s for heaviest annual snowfall two of which being the top, these cities in order are; Aomori in Tohoku, Sapporo in Hokkaido, Toyama in Hokuriku and Akita in Tohoku, some of these recieving up to 312 inches of snow per year.
